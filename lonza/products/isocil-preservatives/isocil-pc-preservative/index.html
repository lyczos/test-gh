Global Regulatory

Europe

  • Listed in Annex V to Regulation EC/1223/2009
  • Not permitted for leave-on products. Maximum use level of 0.0015 % CMI/MI for rinse-off applications

Japan

  • Listed in the positive list
  • Max use level of 0.01% CMI/MI for rinse-off
  • Not permitted in leave-on

US

  • Approved by PCPC for both rinse-off and leave-on applications
  • Max level of 0.00075% CMI/MI (7.5 ppm active ingredients) in leave-on products
  • Max level of 0.0015% CMI/MI (15 ppm active ingredients) in rinse-off products
