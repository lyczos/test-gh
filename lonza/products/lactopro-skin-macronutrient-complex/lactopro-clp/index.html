Clinical Study

Methodology & Protocol
The efficacy of LactoPro CLP was evaluated in a 7-day double blind in vivo study.

15 subjects followed a 5-day washout period before applying two test products (one on each inner forearm) 3 times daily. Skin elasticity and trans-epidermal water loss readings were taken on Day 0, Day 3, Day 5 and Day 7.

The two test products were an oil-in-water emulsion formulated with 5% LactoPro CLP and the same formula base without LactoPro CLP.
